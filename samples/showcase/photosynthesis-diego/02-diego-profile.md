## Notes for tailoring lessons
- When introducing trains, reference his interest in trains: Ask him to explain the difference between steam and diesel engines, drawing on his knowledge of the GP38-2 and SD40-2.
- When introducing dinosaurs, reference his passion for dinosaurs: Have him compare and contrast Allosaurus and Tyrannosaurus, focusing on his preference for Allosaurus.
- When introducing reading, reference his love of encyclopedias: Ask him to explain the difference between the DK Smithsonian "Dinosaurs!" and Trains magazine, highlighting their respective focuses.
