# Homework — Fractions and Ratios

## Practice problems
1. Find an equivalent fraction for 2/3. (maps to: Equivalent Fractions)
2. Explain why the ratio 4:8 simplifies to 2:2. (maps to: Ratios)
3. What is the sum of 1/4 and 2/4? (maps to: Fractions)
4.  A recipe calls for 1/2 cup of flour. If you want to double the recipe, how much flour do you need? (maps to: Fractions)
5.  Simplify the ratio 12:18. (maps to: Ratios)

## Reflection prompt
How does the concept of a ratio relate to the concept of a fraction?
