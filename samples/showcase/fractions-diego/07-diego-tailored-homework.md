# Homework — Fractions and Ratios
## Practice problems
1. Barcelona scored 2 goals out of 3 shots in the first half. What is the fraction of goals scored compared to the number of shots taken? (maps to: Equivalent Fractions)
2. Goku has a power level of 4,000 while Vegeta has 18,000. What is the ratio of Goku's power level to Vegeta's power level? (maps to: Ratios)
3. A recipe calls for 1/4 cup of sugar. What is the sum of 1/4 and 2/4? (maps to: Fractions)
4. A recipe calls for 1/2 cup of flour. If you want to double the recipe, how much flour do you need? (maps to: Fractions)
5. Goku has a power level of 12,000 while Vegeta has 18,000. Simplify the ratio of Goku's power level to Vegeta's power level. (maps to: Ratios)
